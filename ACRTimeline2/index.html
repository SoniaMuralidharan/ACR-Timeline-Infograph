SHOW TIPS


How to Read this Graph?

* The number MM-DD in the boxes represents the date of the letter as Month-Date
* Each Pie represents a Department
* Each dotted Octagon represents a month, the inner one starting from February
* Mouse over the boxes to see more detail on the letter
* Click on the boxes to open the letter itself

Blue Box - a letter from GFoI to concerned department
Green Box - a reply from concerned government department
Gray Box - officials forwarding the letter between various departments
